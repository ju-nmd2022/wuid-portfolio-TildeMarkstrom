Svensk Undulathobby

This is a logotype suggestion for the swedish budgie association.

History
My mother bought her first budgie in 2017, and it was only supposed to be a pet, then she bought another, and another... today in 2022 my mother is one of swedens biggest breeders of budgies. In 2021 she was elected as president of the association. And when I started in this program she asked me to refresh their logo.
Design
The association wanted to keep the bird and his posture, so I traced the original (which was a scanned painting, so you can imagine the blurriness), Then I picked the colors after the Pantone color that the budgie is supposed to be.
I added a sign under the stick the budgie was sitting on and added their name to it. This Logo is specifically made for large print / a large logo. When it's scaled down the name is added next to the budgie, in the same font as on the sign.
